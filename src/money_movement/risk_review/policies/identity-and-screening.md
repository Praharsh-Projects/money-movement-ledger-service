# Identity and Screening Policy

## Screening alerts

Any potential or test-confirmed sanctions screening match must be routed to compliance manual review. The workflow must preserve the alert category, cite policy evidence, and avoid resolving or dismissing the match autonomously.

## Document mismatch

An identity-document mismatch requires priority manual review. The reviewer should validate source-system quality and request approved follow-up evidence through established channels; the model must not invent missing identity facts.

## Data minimization

Model inputs should use tokenized subject references and the minimum facts needed for routing. Names, addresses, raw identity numbers, payment-card numbers, email addresses, phone numbers, and document images must not be sent to the model. Untrusted notes must be redacted before use.
