# Manual Review Policy

## Human decision boundary

The automated workflow may collect facts, retrieve policy evidence, and recommend an operations queue. A trained reviewer remains responsible for any customer-impacting decision, account restriction, transfer hold, or regulatory action. Model text must never be treated as authorization.

## Transaction velocity

Route a case to priority manual review when six or more transfers are observed within one hour. Reviewers should compare the activity with the tokenized account history and must not infer criminal intent from velocity alone.

## New device and value

A new device combined with a transfer value of at least SEK 25,000, represented as 2,500,000 minor units, requires priority manual review. A new device without the value threshold remains contextual evidence rather than an automatic escalation.

## Standard operations queue

Cases without a screening alert, document mismatch, high transaction velocity, or the new-device value threshold may remain in the standard operations queue. The queue is a routing recommendation and does not approve a transfer.
